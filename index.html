About Me

Professor of Electrical & Computer Science Engineering and Dean (Academic Affairs)

With a distinguished career spanning over 28+ years in academia, I am committed to advancing the field of robust control systems through rigorous research and innovative teaching methodologies. My research work focuses on Autonomous systems, Cyber-physical systems, Multi-Agent Systems, Robotics, and AI-enabled Control Systems.

I have completed PhD from IIT Bombay under the supervision of distinguish Professor of Systems and Control Engineering Prof. Bijnan Bandyopadhyay (FIEEE, FNAE, FNASc) and since then contributed significantly to research with 90 publications (journal and conference proceedings) including 5 monographs and over 1000+ citations from 55+ countries. My research work is useful in several civilian applications like Smart/Micro grid, Traffic and Collision Avoidance System, Coordination of Robotic Teams, Drone Swarm and Formation and many more.

As a Dean of Academic Affairs at IITRAM Ahmedabad, I lead the academic programs and offer proactive, data-driven guidance on academic matters, including program offerings, student success, faculty evaluation, and accreditation; I lead system-wide initiatives for student recruitment, retention, and graduation, ensuring accountability and progress tracking, managing the existing academic programs, introducing new programs, curricular development, inter-department coordination, convocation, accreditation and ranking process.
